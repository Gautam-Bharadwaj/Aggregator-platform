# Idea Specification: Vehicle Rental Aggregator

## Overview
A multi-role marketplace that aggregates local car, bike, and scooty rental vendors into one platform, providing users with real-time, verified, and nearest rental options.

## Problem Statement
* **Fragmented Market:** Local vendors lack digital presence.
* **Price Uncertainty:** No standardized pricing for users.
* **Trust Issues:** Lack of verified documents for both vendors and users.
* **Manual Errors:** Inventory managed on paper leads to overbooking.

## Core Solution
A central platform connecting:
1. **Users:** Seeking quick, affordable, and nearby rentals.
2. **Vendors:** Local businesses needing digital tools to manage and grow their fleet.

## Key Pillars
* **Proximity Search:** Shows the nearest available vehicles first.
* **Live Inventory:** Real-time updates to prevent double-booking.
* **Trust Layer:** In-app KYC (Aadhaar/DL) and verified vendor listings.
* **Transparent Payments:** Fixed pricing with secure digital transactions.

## Target Audience
* **Tourists:** For local sightseeing.
* **Students/Professionals:** For temporary daily commuting.
* **Small Vendors:** To digitize their traditional rental business.

## Revenue Model
* **Commission:** Fixed percentage per booking.
* **Featured Listings:** Paid priority for vendors in search results.

## Expected Impact
* **Users:** Faster discovery and guaranteed prices.
* **Vendors:** Increased bookings and automated fleet management.
